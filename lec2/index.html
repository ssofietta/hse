Naive Bayes Classifier (a bit math)

Task: 3 organizations apply reports different number of reports to be checks by the headquarters: 1515, 1010, 2525 respectively. Probabilities of making reports without mistakes are following: 0.90.9, 0.80.8, 0.850.85. One report has been chosen. Find the probability that this report was written by the second organization.

Solution:

Let A1A1, A2A2, A3A3 - events of choosing the report for 3 organizations

So, probabilities: P(A1)=1550P(A1)=1550, P(A2)=1050P(A2)=1050, P(A3)=2550P(A3)=2550

Get full probability: P(B)=0.9⋅1550+0.8⋅1050+0.85⋅2550=0.855P(B)=0.9⋅1550+0.8⋅1050+0.85⋅2550=0.855

Answer: P(A2|B)=0.2⋅0.80.855=0.19P(A2|B)=0.2⋅0.80.855=0.19
